What can you teach?



Our program runs from 3 months to 2 years depending on your application.

At International Education we believe in covering the subjects that will give them the education they will need to progress in their learning. Our teachers need to be able to communicate in English.



Subjects:

At a primary to middle school level.
  • Maths.
  • Science
  • Social studies
  • Art
  • Language arts
  • English
  • Drama

Languages:


We need our teachers to be fluent in English as they are going to be teaching the children in English. It is preferred and recommended that you have a second language as it raises your chances of having a successful application but it isn't mandatory.

Where we recruit from:



As of now we recruit from Europe,the US, Canada and Australia. We are in the process of expanding our reach and recruitment so hopefully very soon we will be able to take applications from around the world.
