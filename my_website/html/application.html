Review our checklist to see if you are eligible:

  2. You need to have no criminal record in any case.
  3. It is necessary that you have or are completing a Bachelors Degree
    in education for the subject you are applying to teach.
  4. If you are currently completing your degree please go to the
    facts page to learn more about your process.
  5. You need to submit documents confirming your health status
    and wellbeing from your local medical centre.
  6. You need to be available to teach full time for at least 1 school year minimum.
