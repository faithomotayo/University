Our beliefs!

At International Education we believe that everyone deserves an education.That is why partnered up with Action Aid to provide an education for students in underdeveloped countries by doing a teacher exchange and sending educators over there to teach in schools that we built.

We believe that it is important to elevate and develop the strong and great minds of tomorrow and you can aid us by signing up to be apart of our teacher exchange!

The Experience:

Growth! Adventure! Global networking!

This program offers a unique and broad range of experience for our employees.
You get to immerse yourself into a whole different culture which enrich your minds about the world while you also get to do what you enjoy and teach simultaneously!
You will also be making a palpable difference in the lives of the people you will be teaching during your program as you will be changing their lives.
At the end of your program you will qualify to receive a certificate that states that you are one of our cultural ambassadors that has encouraged cross-cultural understanding and empathy. As at the end of this experience you should have a deeper understanding of the other cultures values, dilemmas and views.

Our Process:

After you get accepted!

When you receive your invitation to our formidable program we will then guide you through the process to get you started:

  • Applying and receiving your Visa.
  • Flights and accommodation.
  • Online training sessions and support.
  • Professional development
  • Assistance and support during your stay!

There may be more steps we need to go through depending on your application but that will be explained in the email we send after your acceptance email.
